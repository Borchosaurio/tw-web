Temps / An Orangey Tale

Production—Thinkwild Studios
Director—Carlos Gómez-Mira
Madrid, Spain

The Project

An orangey tale about workplace happiness, told as two parallel stories — one of someone unheard and deeply underused at work, the other of someone recognised, motivated, alive. Temps is a small reminder that storytelling, more than any list of policies, is what makes an idea land.
