Kuikma / Hybrid Pro
Coki Nieto

Client — Kuikma
Director — Carlos Gómez-Mira
Madrid, Spain

The Project

We produced a brand spot for Kuikma showcasing their new Hybrid Pro paddle, fronted by professional padel player and brand ambassador Coki Nieto. The production combined studio shoots with on-location footage, and in post-production we integrated AI-driven techniques to push the visual language beyond what traditional workflows allow — blending environments, enhancing motion sequences, and creating a cohesive aesthetic that bridges the real and the crafted.

The result is a film that puts the paddle and the player at the centre of a world built specifically around them.
